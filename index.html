About Me

I am a dedicated and results-driven professional with a strong background in call center operations. I started my career as a dispatcher, where my efficiency and problem-solving skills quickly set me apart. My ability to handle high-pressure situations and streamline processes led to a rapid promotion to Team Leader, where I now guide and motivate my team to deliver exceptional service.

Despite my career in the call center industry, I hold a degree in Botany and Biochemistry, which has sharpened my analytical thinking and attention to detail. My scientific background gives me a unique perspective on process optimization, data analysis, and structured problem-solving—skills I effectively apply in my leadership role.

I am passionate about fostering teamwork, enhancing customer service efficiency, and continuously improving call center operations. Thriving in dynamic environments, I am always eager to take on new challenges that push my team and the business forward.
